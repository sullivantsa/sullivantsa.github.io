What is TSA?

Technology Student Association (TSA) is a teamwork-based group that allows students to share and expand their technology skills while interacting with other students and building intriguing inventions.

What can you find on our website?

On our website, you can find numerous informational data relating to our Chapter and its corresponding activities and events. Specifically, there are links to the Chapter Info, Our Science Program, and a Contact Page. If there is information that cannot be found on this website that you are interested in, don't hesitate to contact us.

Interested in joining, but don't know what we have to offer or where to meet?

TSA sponsors many events and competitions that students can engage in, and we guarantee there is an event that you will enjoy. Visit here to see a complete list of events and descriptions of them. Also, we meet every Tuesday until 4:00 P.M.

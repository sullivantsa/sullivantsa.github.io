Future Advancements for UAS Technology

Drones of tomorrow should be better built so they can be used for all sorts of tasks. They could even be required to endure the most dangerous expeditions.

**For simplicity reasons, the term "drones" will be used instead of Unmanned Aerial Systems.

Future Technological Advancements

Drones of the future could be better built and also harder to crash into obstacles. This could change and be innovated, as we learn more about this technology and innovate it for future uses and needs. Some conmpanies in the future could innovate drones by using sensors to have drones avoid obstacles instead of crash into them and destroying the drone. As we continue to learn more about this technology and innovate it, we can build stronger and more powerful drones. Drones could one day be built to where if you crash on the ground it doesn't completely destroy the drone. Drones overtime could be downsized to fit into more cramped spaces and into smaller parts of caves and caverns. Like cell phones now, drones could get cheaper to own and build as the technology gets cheaper and more accessible to common man. Overall, in the future drones will be improved for future uses and could be more accessible and cheaper for everone to own.

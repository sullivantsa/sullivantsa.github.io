Future Advancements for UAS Technology

Below you can find ways of how UAS Technology is utilized in our society today. Please note that these uses are limited to commercial, technological, and experimental purposes.

**For simplicity reasons, the term "drones" will be used instead of Unmanned Aerial Systems.

Commercial and Personal Uses

In today's high tech world, drones have many beneficial uses for businesses and consumers.

Businesses today can use drones for many marketing and business purposes. A real estate agent can use drones to take aerial videos or photos of a property that is for sale. Real estate agents can show this video on their website with pictures of the property or to see what a property looks like from above. Aircraft companies and people who work on airplanes can use drones to look on top of planes or performs inspections, allowing them to quickly look around the aircraft and analyze problems with ease.

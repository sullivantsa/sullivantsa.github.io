Concerns of UAS Technology

On this page you will encounter concerns and dangers of UAS technology. Please note that these concerns are limited to commercial, technological, and experimental purposes.

**For simplicity reasons, the term "drones" will be used instead of Unmanned Aerial Systems.

General Concerns of UAS Technology

Drones may be useful but during there use there are several concerns with safety.

Though drones maybe useful they do come with there own set of concerns. Concerns of using drones include: fire hazards, crashing, and cutting hazard. Drones are a major fire hazard because if not charged properly the battery can combhust. The fire hazard of drones doesn't just stop at its battery, there is also the chance of en electrical fire in the wiring of the drone. The second hazard of drones is them crashing. Crashing is a frequent concern of using drones. Crashing can be caused by the drone losing the position of the controller. Crashing can also be caused by the drone's or the controller's battery shorting out. Crashing can be very dangers due to the fact that you have a flying piece of metal or plastic falling out of the sky that could cause property damage or damage to personel. The last main concern of using a drone is a cutting hazards. Cutting hazards can be caused from the broken pieces of the drone, if there are any broken fragments on the drone, or the blades of the drone can cut you. Receiving a fleshwound from the drone's blades can be very dangerous depending on the size and depth of the wound because stitches may be required. These are all hazards that you should concern yourself with operating your drone.

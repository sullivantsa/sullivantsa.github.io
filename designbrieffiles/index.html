Brief Overview of UAS Machines

Unmanned Aerial Systems (aka UAS systems) are flying "tools" of technology that allow the user to go to, and view, places that cannot be reached or seen by the naked eye. These machines are sometimes called "drones." Drones have turned into a hobby for most users.

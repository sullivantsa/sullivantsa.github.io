About This Website

This website is for informational and educational purposes based upon the education of uses, concerns, and future advancements about drones (or Unmanned Aerial Systems). The information on this website was methodically thought out and are primary sources and not taken from other websites (unless explicitly stated below in our citations). The images (except for the custom TSA logo) were selectively chosen to best represent the matter at hand, and the owners are given all rights to the photos.
